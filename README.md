# The Isles of Cogdah

A First World War-themed homebrew campaign expansion for Dungeons & Dragons Fifth Edition. Explore the war-torn islands of Cogdah, where five great domains fight for survival against an unholy invasion led by the mysterious Sovereignty of the Three.

## About

**The Unknown War for The Isles of Cogdah** is a comprehensive D&D 5E supplement featuring:
- A rich, centuries-old conflict setting with multiple warring factions
- Custom classes, subclasses, and archetypes
- Period-appropriate equipment and weaponry blending magic and early technology
- The lore of the Five Domains and their final alliance against darkness
